The Client must attend an initial consultation to discuss the design, fabric choices, event date, and estimated budget. Following this, a formal quotation will be issued, detailing the design summary, estimated material cost, and labor fee. This quotation is valid for 14 days.

Should the Client require additional alterations due to weight fluctuations after the final fitting, these will be subject to a separate alteration fee.

3.3. Final Collection

Upon the final fitting and Client's satisfaction, the remaining 50% balance must be paid in full. The garment may only be collected once all outstanding fees are settled.

4.1. Payment Terms
