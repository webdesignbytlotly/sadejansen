Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about our custom design process, appointments, pricing, and timelines.

Yes, all consultations for custom design services are strictly by appointment only. This ensures that you have dedicated, uninterrupted time with Sadé to discuss your vision, measurements, and design details. You can book via the contact form or our booking link.

The pricing for a custom garment varies significantly based on factors like fabric choice (silk, lace, beading), design complexity, and required man-hours. Simple custom designs typically start from **R8,000**, while complex wedding or evening gowns can range higher. A detailed quote is provided after your initial consultation.

For custom bridal gowns, we highly recommend booking **6 to 12 months in advance** of your wedding date. This ensures enough time for sourcing specific materials, pattern development, multiple fittings, and addressing any unexpected delays.

Our standard payment structure is: a **non-refundable 50% deposit** to secure the commission and start the process, and the **remaining 50% balance** is due upon the final fitting and before the garment is collected.

We generally prefer to source the highest quality fabrics ourselves to guarantee the final result. However, we are open to discussing the use of client-supplied fabric, provided the designer approves its quality and suitability for the specific design.

For most complex garments, you can expect between **3 to 5 fittings**. These fittings include the toile/mock-up fitting, the first fabric fitting, and a final fitting for minor adjustments. The exact number depends on the garment's complexity.

Please bring any visual references (photos, sketches), color swatches, and most importantly, the exact **undergarments and shoes** you plan to wear with the final garment. Knowing your budget is also helpful for fabric selection.

Major changes (e.g., changing the silhouette entirely) after the design is approved and the deposit is paid may not be possible or will incur substantial additional costs and delay the completion date. Minor adjustments are expected and managed during fittings.

SADÉ JANSEN focuses exclusively on creating unique, bespoke, custom-made garments for purchase by the client. We do not currently offer a rental service. Please check our 'Shop SADÉ' page for limited ready-to-wear collections.

The design and quote are based on initial measurements. While we account for normal fluctuation, significant body changes (e.g., due to weight loss/gain) after the first fitting may require extensive re-working and will result in an additional alteration fee as per our Terms and Conditions.
