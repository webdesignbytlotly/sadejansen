Our 5-Step Bespoke Journey

1. Consultation

We discuss your vision, event, timeline, and budget. This step solidifies the initial design concept.

2. Sketch & Swatches

SADÉ creates detailed sketches and mood boards, and you select your final luxury fabrics.

3. Pattern & Toile

A mock-up (toile/muslin) is created for your first fitting to perfect the fundamental structure and fit.

4. Creation & Fitting

The garment is constructed from the final material, followed by one or two crucial fittings for final adjustments.

5. Delivery

Your finished, perfectly-tailored bespoke garment is ready for its debut.
